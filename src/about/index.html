About

Statistical Analysis Vizualizions for Introtuctory Statistics (SAVIS) is a collaborative research project on statistical education between CSUS and the School of Mathematics at the University of the Yucatan has led to the creation of a statistical visualization tool that fits precisely the needs of this research project: it contains a menu to illustrate all cases of test of hypothesis and confidence intervals taught in elementary courses (one and two population parameters), it has an online and a stand-alone version (both freely available online), and it is bilingual.
